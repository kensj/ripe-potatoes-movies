Privacy Policy

Ripe Potatoes Promises to only do the Following with your Data:

Transparency

Ripe Potatoes endeavors to be transparent about how we use and store the information we get from our users. Listed in this policy are why and how we collect your information. Additionally, we may update this policy from time to time.



What do we collect?

Ripe Potatoes collects and stores any information that you provide to us during registration as well as any of the preferences you indicate while using the site (ex: Want to See, Not Interested lists). Page history may also be collected, in addition to any content you produce on Ripe Potatoes. We do not collect information about your devices or location.



What do we use this for?

We use this information in order to further personalize your Ripe Potatoes experience. The aforementioned preferences are used to serve you content that you may find more enjoyable than if you were just served random titles. Additionally, we may use these preferences to serve you ads that are more catered to you. Finally, we may use said information internally in aggregations to determine what users like and do not like.



Do we share this information?

For the most part, no. The only information shared are the preference lists. We are an opt-in service, which means that you must opt in if you want your information to be shared with third parties. In special cases like legal cases or transfer of ownership, it is up to our discretion to access, preserve, or disclose such information.
