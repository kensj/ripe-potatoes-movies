FAQ

Some of our most frequently asked questions are here:

Do I need an account?

An account is not needed for some of our services, but some of our most important ones require an account first. To register, click on our login button on the navigation bar, then click on "Register" in the modal that pops up. After submitting a registration form, you should then verify your email.



How can I submit a review?

You can submit a review by going to a specific content page. The text box to enter your review will be on the bottom of the page.



How can I rate content?

Similar to submitting a review, there will be a rating system on each content page. Simply rate however many stars you want, then click on that many.



How can I search for specific content?

You can type your criteria into the search bar in the navigation bar. After entering, you will be taken to an advanced search page where you can refine your search even further.
